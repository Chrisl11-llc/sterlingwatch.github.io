Meet our Team


Welcome to Sterling Watch, your number one source for the most luxurious watches under the sun . We're dedicated to giving you the very best in watches, with a focus on dependability, customer service and high-end quality.

Founded in 2019 by 3 students Sterling Watch has come a long way from its beginnings in at the University of Wisconsin Oshkosh. When we first started out, our passion to provide a safe place to purchase the rarest watches in the world. drove us to build this brand, we turned hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of the upper tier of the watch industry.


We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
